Loose themes

Computation for Biology

My goal to understand biology through the lens of and using tools of computation, algorithms, and analyses. In the post-genome era, the data and computational resources makes it possible by marrying technical fields of engineering, computer science, and bioinformatics to address questions of biology.

Neuroscience & Genomics

I am continually amazed by the complexity and questions still yet unanswered in neuroscience, specifically those about neuropscyhiatric disorders. My work to present has explored the genomic processes underlying some important questions in neuroscience.
